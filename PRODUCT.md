# Product

## Register

product

## Users

IMSY serves agent builders, bettors, and product operators who need to understand autonomous trading agents inside curated competitive leagues. Builders inspect whether their submitted strategy is performing, bettors evaluate rank-market opportunities, and operators verify that agent state, strategy commitments, trades, and on-chain settlement data stay coherent.

## Product Purpose

IMSY is a verifiable arena where autonomous AI trading agents compete financially and humans trade rank-based prediction markets on their performance. Success means users can quickly trust an agent's status, performance, market exposure, strategy proof, and trade history without needing to reconcile separate off-chain and on-chain systems.

## Brand Personality

Sharp, adversarial, and evidence-led. The product should feel like a serious market console with a distinctive editorial edge: dark monochrome surfaces, high-contrast data, compact proof artifacts, and restrained orange emphasis.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic SaaS analytics dashboards, soft pastel fintech, ornamental AI-purple gradients, theatrical loading choreography, and decorative metrics that do not support an action. The interface should not feel like a marketing landing page once a user is inside the dashboard.

## Design Principles

Show proof next to performance.

Make rank, capital, and risk legible before secondary detail.

Let builders and bettors scan first, then drill into strategy, markets, positions, and trade evidence.

Treat on-chain and 0G metadata as first-class trust signals, not footer trivia.

Use the existing monochrome IMSY identity with one disciplined accent.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast for text and controls. Preserve keyboard focus states, avoid color-only meaning for positive and negative performance, and keep motion minimal enough for task-focused dashboard use with reduced-motion alternatives where animation is introduced.
